This project analyzes marketing campaign performance data to evaluate the effectiveness of campaigns across different channels, regions, and time periods.

üîπ Business Problem

Marketing teams often run multiple campaigns simultaneously but lack clarity on:

Which campaigns deliver the highest return on investment (ROI)

Which channels and locations perform best

Whether higher spending actually leads to better engagement and profitability

Objective:
Analyze campaign performance metrics to optimize marketing spend and improve overall ROI.

üîπ Key Insights

Certain marketing channels consistently deliver higher ROI than others

High acquisition cost does not always result in higher engagement or profitability

Campaign performance varies significantly by location and campaign type

Seasonal trends influence overall marketing effectiveness

üîπ Business Recommendations

Increase budget allocation to consistently high-ROI channels

Reduce or redesign campaigns with persistent negative ROI

Optimize campaigns with high cost but low engagement scores

Use location-based and seasonal insights for targeted marketing strategies

Track ROI as a primary KPI instead of impressions or clicks alone
